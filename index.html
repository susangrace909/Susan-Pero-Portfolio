About Me

Hello! My name is Susan Pero and I am from Orlando, Florida. I grew up in a musical household and originally went to school to study music education. I taught elementary music, but chose to change gears and follow my passion for plants. Once I completed my degrees in Plant Science I began to work as head grower for a local cactus nursery. I loved what I did, but knew that such a physical career would not be sustainable. While working in those fields I dabbled in coding and even did some free bootcamps, so I chose to look into making it my career. I am currently attending UCF's Coding Bootcamp with hopes of a job upon completion.
